White Sauce Pasta

Preparation Time: 25 Minutes

Difficulty Level: Easy

Category: Italian

Ingredients

  • 2 cups Pasta
  • 2 tbsp Butter
  • 2 tbsp Maida (Flour)
  • 2 cups Milk
  • 1/2 cup Cheese
  • 1 Onion
  • 1 Capsicum
  • 1/2 cup Sweet Corn
  • Black Pepper as per taste
  • Salt as per taste
  • Chilli Flakes and Oregano

Instructions

  1. Boil the pasta in salted water until soft.
  2. Heat butter in a pan and add chopped onion and capsicum.
  3. Add sweet corn and cook for 2 minutes.
  4. Add flour and stir continuously.
  5. Slowly pour milk and mix well to avoid lumps.
  6. Add cheese, black pepper, salt, oregano, and chilli flakes.
  7. Add the boiled pasta and mix everything properly.
  8. Cook for 2 to 3 minutes and serve hot.
