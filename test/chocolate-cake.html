Chocolate Cake

Preparation Time: 45 Minutes

Difficulty Level: Medium

Category: Dessert

Ingredients

  • 2 cups Flour
  • 1 cup Sugar
  • 1/2 cup Cocoa Powder
  • 1 tsp Baking Powder
  • 1/2 tsp Baking Soda
  • 2 Eggs
  • 1 cup Milk
  • 1/2 cup Butter
  • 1 tsp Vanilla Essence
  • Chocolate Syrup for topping

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 180°C.
  2. Mix flour, cocoa powder, baking powder, and baking soda in a bowl.
  3. In another bowl, beat eggs, sugar, butter, milk, and vanilla essence.
  4. Add the dry ingredients to the wet mixture and mix well.
  5. Pour the batter into a greased baking tray.
  6. Bake for 30 to 35 minutes.
  7. Check with a toothpick if the cake is fully baked.
  8. Allow it to cool and pour chocolate syrup on top.
  9. Slice and serve.
