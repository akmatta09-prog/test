Paneer Butter Masala

Preparation Time: 30 Minutes

Difficulty Level: Easy

Category: Veg

Ingredients

  • 200g Paneer
  • 2 Tomatoes
  • 1 Onion
  • 2 tbsp Butter
  • 1/2 cup Fresh Cream
  • 1 tsp Ginger Garlic Paste
  • 1 tsp Red Chilli Powder
  • 1/2 tsp Turmeric Powder
  • 1 tsp Garam Masala
  • Salt as per taste
  • Fresh Coriander for garnish

Instructions

  1. Heat butter in a pan.
  2. Add chopped onions and cook until golden brown.
  3. Add ginger garlic paste and cook for 1 minute.
  4. Add chopped tomatoes and cook until soft.
  5. Add turmeric, red chilli powder, garam masala, and salt.
  6. Blend the mixture into a smooth gravy.
  7. Put the gravy back into the pan and add fresh cream.
  8. Add paneer cubes and cook for 5 minutes.
  9. Garnish with coriander and serve hot.
