Veg Sandwich

Preparation Time: 15 Minutes

Difficulty Level: Easy

Category: Veg Snack

Ingredients

  • 4 Bread Slices
  • 1 Boiled Potato
  • 1 Tomato
  • 1 Cucumber
  • 1 Onion
  • 2 tbsp Butter
  • 2 tbsp Green Chutney
  • Salt as per taste
  • Black Pepper as per taste
  • Cheese Slices (optional)

Instructions

  1. Apply butter and green chutney on the bread slices.
  2. Place sliced potato, tomato, cucumber, and onion on one bread slice.
  3. Sprinkle salt and black pepper over the vegetables.
  4. Add a cheese slice if desired.
  5. Cover with another bread slice.
  6. Toast the sandwich in a sandwich maker or pan until golden brown.
  7. Cut into halves and serve with ketchup or chutney.
